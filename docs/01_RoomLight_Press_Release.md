# RoomLight

### New satisfying way to control lighting!

If you own or manage a hotel, you'll know this feeling: You have to quickly turn the lights on or off in a specific floor or certain rooms, but shutting off the electricity will only cause more problems.

Light switches are notoriously difficult to get right, but we really believe that we've made something truly special.

RoomLight is responsive and easy to use: Synchronize multiple lights to one controller and control them from anywhere within the building!

At our annual expo, one attendee told our marketing team: "At first I was sceptical, but once I tried RoomLight for the first time, I already knew I would never use a normal light switch again."
